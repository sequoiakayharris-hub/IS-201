My Travels!

My Favorite Places:

  • New Zealand
  • Uganda
  • Italy

New Zealand

I absolutely loved New Zealand and I would go back in a heart beat! Here is why:

  • Amazing views with everything from mountains, to beaches, to glaciers
  • Slower pace of life that made you feel less stress
  • Full of adventure

Uganda

We went to Uganda for a service trip and I loved it! Here is why:

  • The kindest people ever
  • The landscape was beautiful and had so many cool animals
  • There is so many opportunities for service

Italy

I went to Italy with my bestfriend Karis for our Senior trip which was so fun! Here is why:

  • The food was to die for
  • There was a lot of history and amazing art
  • We laughed the whole trip!
